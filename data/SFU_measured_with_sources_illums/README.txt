Dataset of illuminants taken from:

https://www2.cs.sfu.ca/~colour/data/colour_constancy_synthetic_test_data/index.html

Here is the description of the dataset:
The second illuminant set (measured_with_sources.illum) is a larger set of measured spectra. 
This set consists of 81 spectra measured in and around the SFU campus at various times of the day, and in a variety of weather conditions. 
Unusual lighting, such as that beside neon advertising lights, was excluded. However, care was taken to include some reflected light, provided that it was not too extreme.
